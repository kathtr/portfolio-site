About

I’m Katharina Treptow, a UI/UX Designer from Germany, currently based in Amsterdam, the Netherlands. With a background in Business Administration, I jumpstarted my career as a IT Project Coordinator back in 2018, where I soon found myself striving for a change.

I’ve always been an empathetic personality and creative mind, that is why a very good friend called my attention to UI/UX Design (and I’m forever grateful for that). Since then, I’ve signed up to a bootcamp and worked on my portfolio. Additionally, I’m currently developing my skills in Front-End Development to complement my Design skills.

I am looking forward to working in an innovative company culture where I can further grow my interpersonal and technical skills by helping achieve meaningful design goals.
